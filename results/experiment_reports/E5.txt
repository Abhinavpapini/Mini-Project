=====================================================================
KEY PROJECT CONCLUSIONS (40 EXPERIMENTS)
=====================================================================

1. DUAL-SSL IS THE #1 DRIVER: HuBERT+Whisper combination adds +0.1 F1
   over best single-SSL model (F3=0.540 → C4=0.659)

2. FUSION MECHANISM IS SECONDARY: ±0.01 F1 between XAttn/Graph-T/Atrous

3. SPEAKER DISENTANGLEMENT (F3-GRL) IS THE BEST SINGLE-MODEL APPROACH:
   0.540 F1 with only HuBERT+PCA-32 — the best "lightweight" model.

4. SSL+MFCC (D6) IS THE BEST BALANCED MODEL: All 5 classes above 0.54,
   best production choice when per-class fairness matters.

5. ICA IS CATEGORICALLY WRONG for stutter features (0.276 F1).

6. AUGMENTATION HURTS FOR FEATURES (F2=0.434, F5=0.446):
   Must apply augmentation at raw audio, not cached feature level.

7. OVERFITTING IS UNIVERSAL at 1M+ params with 20K samples:
   All post-35 models best at ep2-4. Proper regularisation could push
   C4 → 0.70+ F1.
